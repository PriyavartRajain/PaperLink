Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share a link to PaperLink?

In any app (Safari, YouTube, etc.), tap the Share button and select PaperLink from the share sheet. A unique 6-digit code will be generated instantly.

How do I access my saved links?

Open the PaperLink app and go to the Scanner tab. Enter your 6-digit code and tap "Open Link" to access your saved content.

Can I see all my saved links?

Yes! Go to the History tab to see all your saved links with their titles and codes. You can tap any link to open it directly.

Are my links synced across devices?

Currently, links are stored locally on each device. We're considering adding iCloud sync in a future update.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your links are stored locally on your device. We don't collect, track, or share any of your data.

What if I forget a code?

No worries! You can always check the History tab to see all your saved links and their corresponding codes.
