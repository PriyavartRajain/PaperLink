Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At PaperLink, we believe your privacy is fundamental. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Don't Collect

PaperLink is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. Specifically:

  • We don't collect your links or URLs
  • We don't track your browsing habits
  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't use analytics or tracking services
  • We don't share data with third parties

Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Links and Codes: Your saved links and their corresponding 6-digit codes are stored in a local database on your device
  • App Groups: We use iOS App Groups to share data between the main app and the share extension, but this data never leaves your device
  • Metadata: When we fetch webpage titles, this is done directly from your device to the website - we never see or store this information

Network Requests

The only network requests made by PaperLink are:

  • Metadata Fetching: When you save a link, your device may request the webpage title directly from the source website to display a meaningful title in your history
  • These requests are made directly from your device to the website - they don't go through our servers
  • We don't log, store, or have access to these requests

Third-Party Services

PaperLink uses Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • LinkPresentation: Apple's framework for fetching webpage metadata
  • Core Data: Apple's framework for local data storage
  • Share Extensions: Apple's framework for receiving shared content

These frameworks operate according to Apple's privacy policies and don't send data to PaperLink's developers.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security features
  • Data is encrypted when your device is locked (iOS automatic encryption)
  • No data is transmitted over the internet to our servers

Children's Privacy

PaperLink is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect contact information, we recommend checking this page periodically for updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@paperlink.app

Privacy Summary

In simple terms: PaperLink stores your links locally on your device and never sends them to us or anyone else. Your privacy is completely protected because we never see your data.
